## WOR-110

Added the Login page (`/login`) and Profile page (`/profile`). The login
page presents a centered card with email-based magic-link sign-in and
Google OAuth, inline validation errors, and a confirmation state after
sending the magic link. The profile page lets users view their email
(read-only), edit their display name, and sign out. Protected routes now
redirect back to the originally requested page after login instead of
always landing on `/dashboard`.
