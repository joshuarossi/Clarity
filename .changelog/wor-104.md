## WOR-104 — Shared chat components

Added the four foundational chat UI components in `src/components/chat/`:
**ChatWindow**, **MessageBubble**, **MessageInput**, and
**StreamingIndicator**. These provide a consistent, accessible
conversation experience across Private Coaching, Joint Chat, and Draft
Coach surfaces — including seven bubble variants (user, coach-private,
coach-joint, coach-intervention, party-initiator, party-invitee, error),
three message statuses (streaming, complete, error), sticky auto-scroll,
Enter-to-send input, hover timestamps, and a blinking streaming cursor.
