## WOR-165: Disable E2E job in CI to unblock auto-deploy

The Playwright E2E job in `.github/workflows/ci.yml` has been commented out.
The deferred Playwright suite was failing CI on every push to `main`, which
blocked the auto-deploy trigger and forced manual workflow dispatch. The job
definition is preserved as a comment so it can be re-enabled once the
Playwright suite is stable.
