## WOR-144 — Coach opening message on joint session entry

When a party enters the joint session and the case transitions to
JOINT_ACTIVE for the first time, the AI Coach now automatically posts a
contextual opening message grounded in the case's main topic. This
fulfils PRD acceptance criterion US-09b ("the Coach AI posts an opening
message grounded in the case's main topic") so that parties no longer
enter an empty chat room with no facilitation.
