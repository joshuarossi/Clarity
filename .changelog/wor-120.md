### WOR-120 — Solo mode party toggle

Solo cases now display a "Viewing As" segmented toggle in the page header,
letting a single user switch between the Initiator and Invitee perspectives
without a second browser session. The selected party is stored in the URL
(`?as=initiator|invitee`) so it survives page refreshes, and all downstream
queries — private coaching messages, party state, AI responses — resolve
against the chosen party.
