## WOR-113

Added the **New Case** page (`/cases/new`) — a progressive-disclosure form
that walks initiators through category selection (radio cards), topic,
private description, desired outcome, and other-party naming before calling
the `cases/create` mutation. Privacy-sensitive fields display a lock icon
with tooltip, the topic field enforces a soft 140-character limit with a
live counter, and an expandable "Advanced" section exposes a solo-mode
toggle. The form is fully keyboard-navigable and WCAG AA compliant.
