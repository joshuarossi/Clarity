# WOR-130 — Closure UI: three-path modal + confirmation banner

Added case closure UI to joint chat. A "Close" button in the top nav opens
a styled modal with three paths: **Resolved** (propose resolution with a
summary for the other party to confirm), **Not resolved** (unilateral
immediate close), and **Take a break** (close the tab, case stays active).
When one party proposes resolution, the other party sees a confirmation
banner with the summary and can accept or reject. Confirming transitions
the case to CLOSED_RESOLVED; rejecting clears the proposal and keeps the
session open.
