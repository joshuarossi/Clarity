## WOR-114

Added the **Invite Sharing** page (`/cases/:caseId/invite`) — after creating
a standard case the initiator lands here to share the invite link with the
other party. The page provides a prominent monospace link field with one-click
copy, three contextual share options (email with pre-written message, short
text-friendly snippet, and raw link), an expandable "What should I tell them?"
section with suggested language, and a secondary CTA to start private coaching
immediately. Solo-mode cases bypass this page and route straight to coaching.
