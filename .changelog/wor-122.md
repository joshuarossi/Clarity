### WOR-122 — Synthesis AI action with privacy filter

After both parties complete private coaching, the system now automatically
generates personalised synthesis guidance for each party using Claude Sonnet.
A token-based privacy filter ensures neither party's raw words are leaked to
the other; on repeated filter failures the system falls back to a generic
synthesis and flags the case for admin review. On success the case transitions
atomically to READY_FOR_JOINT.
