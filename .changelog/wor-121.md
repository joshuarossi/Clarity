### WOR-121 — Case detail orchestrator page

The `/cases/:caseId` route now serves as the central orchestrator for the
entire case experience. It reactively reads the case status from Convex and
renders the appropriate subview (Private Coaching, Ready for Joint, Joint
Chat, or Closed) without requiring page navigation. Subroutes (`/private`,
`/joint`, `/closed`) redirect automatically if the case status doesn't match,
and non-party users are redirected to the dashboard with an error toast.
