# WOR-125 — Coach AI action: Haiku gate + Sonnet generation with streaming

The AI Coach now actively participates in joint chat sessions. When a party
sends a message, a fast Haiku classification step determines whether the Coach
should respond (inflammatory remarks, meaningful progress, or direct questions).
If intervention is warranted, Sonnet generates a context-aware response streamed
in real time, with a privacy filter ensuring no raw private-session content
leaks into the shared conversation.
